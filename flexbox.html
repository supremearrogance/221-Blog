Reflection: Implementing Responsive Design

Shown above are the potential wireframes for this website if they were to be displayed on an iPad or iPhone Device.

Implementing Responsive Web Design allows websites that are projected on a Computer to be resized to suit the dimensions of smaller devices. This makes it more friendly for users who prefer to operate with their phones - ultimately making their experience on the particular website better. From a business standpoint, satisfied website users means that they may be more inclined to purchase their products which would see increased sales and revenue for the business. Responsive Design may take a little bit to grasp, but when implemented correctly, it can do wonders for a website.
